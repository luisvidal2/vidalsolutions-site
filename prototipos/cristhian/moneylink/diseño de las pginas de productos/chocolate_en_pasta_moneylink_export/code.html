Our Heritage

Crafting Legacy from the Amazonian Floor

For generations, the families of Alto Beni have nurtured the wild cacao trees that thrive in the rich, humid soils of the Bolivian Amazon. This is not just chocolate; it is a cultural artifact.

Moneylink bridges this ancestral knowledge with the rigorous standards of international connoisseurs, ensuring that every batch of paste retains its soul while meeting the highest export quality.
