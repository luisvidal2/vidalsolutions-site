BOLIVIAN ORIGIN

Quinua Real:
The Ancestral Grain

Grown at 3,700 meters above sea level, our Royal Quinoa is hand-harvested following centuries of Altiplano tradition.

OUR LEGACY

Ancestral Heritage

For over 5,000 years, the indigenous communities of the Andes have cultivated Quinua Real. This isn't just a product; it's a sacred link to the past, nurtured by the unique mineral composition of the Uyuni salt flats and the intense solar radiation of the high-altitude plains.

location_on Bolivian Altiplano: 3,700 MSNM

The Micro-Climate of Uyuni

The Salar Effect

The proximity to the world's largest salt flats creates a soil rich in potassium and lithium, naturally enhancing the nutritional density and size of the Royal grain compared to standard quinoa varieties.
