OUR LEGACY

The Heritage of the Altiplano

Born in the heart of La Paz over 130 years ago, Paceña is more than a beer; it is the liquid soul of Bolivia. In the high-altitude air of the Andes, where water is purer and oxygen is thinner, we perfected a pilsener that defied standard brewing physics.

Moneylink proudly brings this heritage export to the international stage, maintaining the strict standards set by the founding masters of the Cervecería Boliviana Nacional.
