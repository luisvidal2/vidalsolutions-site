Organic Royal Quinoa

Royal quinoa is the most valued variety worldwide, grown exclusively in the southern Altiplano of Bolivia, around the Salar de Uyuni. Its large grain, fluffy texture, and exceptional nutritional profile make it the superfood par excellence.

Processed under strict quality standards for absolute purity. Recognized worldwide for its nutritional value and unique flavor, ideal for the demanding Japanese market.
